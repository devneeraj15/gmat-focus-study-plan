Quant Trap Guardrails

  • ⚠️ Inequalities sign flips: Always flip the inequality sign when multiplying/dividing by a negative number.
  • ⚠️ Consecutive sums: Remember odd/even counts in ranges. For consecutive ranges, count is \(Last - First + 1\).
  • ⚠️ Prime divisors: Don't guess prime factor counts; map them systematically using factor trees.

Verbal Section (23 Questions | 45 Mins)

Critical Reasoning (CR) and Reading Comprehension (RC) only. No Sentence Correction.

Critical Reasoning (CR) Strategy

  • Pre-thinking: Brainstorm assumptions/gaps before looking at answer options.
  • Role of Bolded Parts: Categorize lines as evidence, intermediate conclusion, or main conclusion.
  • Common Traps: Avoid out-of-scope options or answers that weaken when requested to strengthen.

Reading Comprehension (RC) Strategy

  • Paragraph Maps: Briefly summarize the role of each paragraph in 1 sentence while reading.
  • Main Idea: Identify primary purpose based on passage shifts, not isolated paragraphs.
  • Inference: Choose options directly supported by text, avoiding assumptions.
